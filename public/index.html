WHO LIKES PIE?!

I have had a passion for baking since I was a little girl. I started following my mother while she baked, assisting her in what I was allowed. Then I began trying to recreate the recipes myself. Now that I am an adult and a mother, I like to bake pies myself and share them with others. There is an extremely large number to the different kinds of pie you can make. My favorite are a few fruits, such as:

  1. Apple
  2. Blueberry
  3. Peach

I also enjoy Pumpkin or Pecan. You can always make a pie unique with how much crust you put on it, or different spices you put on the crust. I intend to continue making pies for the rest of my life, and sharing my pie passion with others! I like to bake them as thank-yous for people, as welcoming gifts, or as holiday treats!
